To continue using the WhaTap monitoring service after the free trial period has passed or exceeded the limit of free trial terms, register your payment information and convert to a paid service.

## Payment information {#register-pay}

Payment information is provided differently depending on the billing currency. The user must have the <Cmdname sid="billing" className="b600" /> role to register the payment information.

Prepare the following before proceeding with payment.

- **KRW**

  - <Cmdname sid="payment_type_card_iamport" className="uitext" />: Payment card number and its expiration date
  - <Cmdname sid="payment_type_banktransfer" className="uitext" />: Email address for receipt of business registration certificate and tax invoice

- **JPY**, **USD**

  - <Cmdname sid="payment_type_card_stripe" className="uitext" />: Payment card number and its expiration date
  - <Cmdname sid="payment_type_banktransfer_abroad" className="uitext" />: Recipient Email

:::note

For more information about the <Cmdname sid="billing" className="b600" /> role, see [the following](../project/project-structure#project-auth).

:::

### Registering the payment information

If this is your first registration for the payment method, keep the following steps:

1. In [WhaTap monitoring service](https://service.whatap.io), log in with the account to transfer the <Cmdname sid="billing" className="b600" /> role to.

2. Select the profile icon on the upper right of Home screen.

3. If the dropdown menu appears, select <Cmdname sid="my_usage" className="uitext" />.

   <ImgLang img='billing-select-my-usage.png' desc='Usage' />

4. In the <Cmdname sid="payment_information" className="uitext" /> section, select <Cmdname sid="register_billing_info" className="uitext" />.

5. Configure the detailed settings in order. <span class="vslow">\*</span> is required.

   <ImgLang img="billing-payment-information.png" desc="Payment information" />

6. Register a desired payment method in <Cmdname sid="payment_method" className="uitext" />.

   - [<Cmdname sid="payment_type_card_iamport" className="uitext" />](#payment_type_card_iamport)
   - [<Cmdname sid="payment_type_banktransfer" className="uitext" />](#payment_type_banktransfer)
   - [<Cmdname sid="payment_type_card_stripe" className="uitext" />](#payment_type_card_stripe)
   - [<Cmdname sid="payment_type_banktransfer_abroad" className="uitext" />](#payment_type_banktransfer_abroad)

:::note

Supported payment currencies are <UI>KRW</UI>, <UI>JPY</UI>, and <UI>USD</UI>. <Cmdname sid="payment_method" className="uitext" /> is changed depending on the changed currency.

:::

### Credit card (Nicepay) {#payment_type_card_iamport}

If you selected <Cmdname sid="payment_type_card_iamport" className="uitext" /> in <Cmdname sid="payment_method" className="uitext" />, keep the following steps:

<ImgLang img="billing-add-payment-credit.png" desc="Credit card" />

1. When the <Cmdname sid="register_billing_info" className="uitext" /> window appears, enter the values for card number, valid thru, and first 2 digits of password.

2. In <Cmdname sid="card_type" className="uitext" />, select <Cmdname sid="corporation" className="uitext" /> or <Cmdname sid="individual" className="uitext" />.

   - <Cmdname sid="corporation" className="uitext" />: In case of a corporate card, the company name is printed on the card. Enter the business registration number and busines name.
   - <Cmdname sid="individual" className="uitext" />: In case of a personal card, the owner name is printed on the card. Even if you received it from your company but a personal name is printed on it, it is a personal card. Enter 8 digits for your birthday.

3. See the checked terms and conditions.

4. Once you have entered all your card information, select <Cmdname sid="apply" className="uitext" />. Return to the previous screen.

5. Enter the contact information to receive the invoice, and then select <Cmdname sid="save" className="uitext" />.

### Bank transfer {#payment_type_banktransfer}

If you selected <Cmdname sid="payment_type_banktransfer" className="uitext" /> in <Cmdname sid="payment_method" className="uitext" />, keep the following steps:

<ImgLang img="billin-add-payment-tax.png" desc="Bank transfer" />

1. When the <Cmdname sid="register_billing_info" className="uitext" /> window appears, enter the billing information.

   - Enter the representative name, business registration number, and e-tax invoice email on the business registration certificate.

   - The tax invoice issuance date is as follows:

     - <Cmdname sid="date.25" className="uitext" />: Invoice is issued on the 25th of the month.
     - <Cmdname sid="date.-1" className="uitext" />: Invoice is issued on the last day of the month.
     - <Cmdname sid="date.0" className="uitext" />: Invoice is issued at the beginning of the following month based on the used month.

   - If necessary, enter the item and type of the business.

2. Once you have entered all your billing information, select <Cmdname sid="apply" className="uitext" />. Return to the previous screen.

3. Enter the contact information to receive the invoice, and then select <Cmdname sid="save" className="uitext" />.

Once you have completed entry of the invoice issuance information, the copies for WhaTap Labs' business registration certificate and bankbook are automatically sent via the email to the person in charge. Please check the content of the email and then send your business registration certificate to billing@whatap.io.

:::note

- Enter your business registration number and then select <Cmdname sid="BTN06501" className="uitext" /> to proceed with authentication.

- To receive invoices by multiple email addresses, enter multiple email addresses by using comma (,) as the delimiter in <Cmdname sid="invoice_cc" className="uitext" />. e.g. a@whatap.io,b@whatap.io,c@whatap.io

- To reversely issue a tax invoice, contact us at billing@whatap.io.

:::

## Modifying the payment information

You can change your registered payment method or billing currency.

1. Select the profile icon on the upper right of Home screen.

2. If the dropdown menu appears, select <Cmdname sid="my_usage" className="uitext" />.

3. In the <Cmdname sid="payment_information" className="uitext" /> section of the <Cmdname sid="side_paymentInformation" className="uitext" /> menu, select ![Edit icon](/img/ico-edit.svg) <Cmdname sid="edit" className="uitext" />.

   - If you have modified <Cmdname sid="invoice_currency" className="uitext" />, the payment method options are changed.

   - To change a registered credit card to another one, select <Cmdname sid="BTN08031" className="uitext" />.

     <ImgLang img="billing-edit-payment-info-credit.png" desc="Re-register" />

## How to calculate the usage fee of WhaTap products {#countbilling}

WhaTap products are using the deferred payment billing. You can receive an invoice for the previous month's usage within 5 business days of each month via email through the account with the <Cmdname sid="billing" className="b600" /> role. The usage of the previous month is the amount used from the 1st day to the last day of the month. Invoices are created for each account based on the list of paid products registered to the account.

If the subscription period for paid products is in the middle of the month, the period of use is calculated and billed on a pro-rata basis.

## Data retention period

The data retention periods for each WhaTap product are provided as follows:

- RAW data (real-time data) retention period: **1 month**

- Statistical data (5 min/1 hour) retention period: **1 year**

- Profile data retention period: **1 month**

:::note

- In case of the **Network** product, the statistical data (5 min/1 hour) retention period is **100 days**.

- If the log monitoring feature is enabled, the default retention period for log data is **1 day**. You can change it to a desired period. For more information, see [the following](../log/log-setting#log-data-setting).

:::
